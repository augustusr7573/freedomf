Different type of Statistics

Examples

The most powerful messages combine statistics and stories. One powerful statistic is better than three weak ones Ensure your statistics are relevant, interesting, and clear Consider using both “bad” and “good” statistics

  • Example: Bad - In our county, 60% of children in 3rd grade are not reading at grade level.
  • Example: Good - The library’s new early literacy training is reaching 95% of the child care providers in our county.
